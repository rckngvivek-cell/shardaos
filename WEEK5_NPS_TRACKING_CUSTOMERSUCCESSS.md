## 🎯 NPS STRATEGY (Net Promoter Score)

### What is NPS?
**Formula:** % of promoters - % of detractors = NPS score

**Scoring:**
```
9-10 = Promoter (delighted, will recommend)
7-8  = Passive (satisfied but neutral)
0-6  = Detractor (unhappy, might complain)

Week 5 Target: 9.2/10 average across all users
```

### Why NPS?
- **Simple:** Single question captures satisfaction
- **Actionable:** Follow-up questions explain why
- **Comparable:** Benchmark against industry (SaaS avg 30-50)
- **Predictive:** High NPS → retention, referrals, upsells

## 🎯 DETRACTOR ESCALATION PROTOCOL

### If NPS = 0-6 (SAME DAY ACTION)

**Step 1: Immediate Phone Call (Within 2 hours)**
- [ ] Call school admin directly
- [ ] Apologize for poor experience
- [ ] Ask: "What went wrong? How can I fix it?"
- [ ] Take detailed notes

**Sample Script:**
```
"Hi [Name], I saw your feedback on our survey and I'm concerned. 
I want to make this right. What happened that led to the score?"

[Listen, don't defend]

"I understand. Here's what I can do by tomorrow: [specific fix]. 
Can we schedule a call at 3 PM tomorrow to confirm it's resolved?"
```

**Step 2: Root Cause Analysis (Within 24 hours)**
- [ ] Log issue in support system
- [ ] Assign to Backend/Frontend team
- [ ] Escalate to Lead Architect
- [ ] Create 48-hour fix plan

**Step 3: Remediation (Within 48 hours)**
- [ ] Fix deployed
- [ ] Call school to confirm
- [ ] Offer compensation (discount, extra features)

**Step 4: Follow-up NPS (48-72 hours)**
- [ ] Send follow-up survey
- [ ] Target: Convert detractor to passive (7-8) minimum

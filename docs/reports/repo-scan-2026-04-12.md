## Overall Assessment

- The repo is usable as a TypeScript scaffold for shared contracts, an Express API, and a Vite web shell.
- The owner-plane addition is the most actively developed slice in the current working tree.
- Documentation and infrastructure are much broader than the actual runnable product and should be treated as planning material until validated.
- The fastest reliability improvements would be:
  1. fix missing test setup files
  2. align route/port/health endpoint contracts across app, docs, and deployment configs
  3. remove or restore missing-file references
  4. decide whether `deerflow` or `school-erp/shardaos` is the canonical product identity
  5. either validate Terraform or clearly mark it as draft architecture

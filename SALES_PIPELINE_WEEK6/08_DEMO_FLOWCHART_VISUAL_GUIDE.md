## DEMO MUST-HAVES (Tech Checklist)

Before EVERY call:
- [ ] Internet speed tested (min 5 Mbps)
- [ ] Firestore demo data loaded (realistic, school-like data)
- [ ] Screen resolution 1920x1080 (or higher for readability)
- [ ] Zoom/Google Meet link tested (launch 5 min early)
- [ ] Phone on silent (no interruptions)
- [ ] 2nd screen open with ROI calculator (paste link after call)
- [ ] Pricing card printed (show during call if needed)
- [ ] Testimonial video queued (255-sec clip from pilot school)

## SUCCESS RATE BY MODULE

Based on 20+ school demos:
- Reporting module: 95% say "impressive" (biggest wow-factor)
- Parent portal: 88% identify immediate ROI
- SMS alerts: 82% mention adoption concern ("will teachers use this?")
- Analytics: 70% say "need this" (but less convincing than reporting)

**Critical:** Always open with REPORTING (mod 2) to hook them early if time is short.

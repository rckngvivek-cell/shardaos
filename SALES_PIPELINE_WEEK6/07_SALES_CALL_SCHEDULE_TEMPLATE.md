## 🎬 CALL SCRIPT (Use for each demo)

#### Opening (2 min)
```
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking my call. I know you're busy, so I'll be quick.

We work with schools like [School Name] to cut admin time in half and reduce parent 
calls by 70%. I'd like to show you how in 15 minutes [PAUSE for response].

I'm going to screen-share our platform. You'll see real data from a school like yours. 
Fair enough?"
```

#### Problem Validation (5 min)
```
"Before I jump into feature walkthrough, I want to make sure I understand your pain.

Quick questions:
1. How much time does your team spend on generating term reports?
2. How many parent calls do you get per day asking about grades/attendance?
3. Do you have a dedicated IT person maintaining your current system?

[Listen + make notes. Connect pain to solution later]"
```

#### Demo Walkthrough (25 min)
```
"Great. So here's what I'm about to show you:

1. Dashboard (30 sec): Real-time view of your school
2. Reporting (2 min): Generate 20 report types in 30 seconds (vs your current time)
3. Parent Portal (3 min): Parents log in, see grades + attendance (no calls)
4. Mobile App (2 min): Students check grades
5. SMS Alerts (2 min): Automated parent notifications
6. Analytics (3 min): Data-driven decisions

All of this runs on the cloud (no servers to buy), costs ₹2.5L/year, and is live in 2 days.

Let me walk you through screen-share... [START SCREEN SHARE]"

[Walk through each module, relating back to their pain points from earlier]
```

#### Objection Handling (7 min)
```
Common objections:

"Will it take 6 months like PowerSchool?"
→ "Actually, you're live in 2 days. Literally. Day 1: admin setup + data upload. 
   Day 2: teacher training + go-live. We've done this 20 times."

"Is it secure?"
→ "Bank-grade encryption. Daily automated backups. Cloud-hosted, so zero server maintenance.
   Compliant with GDPR. In 20 schools, we've had zero data incidents."

"What if teachers don't adopt it?"
→ "From our 20 schools, adoption is 95% by month 2. We provide 4 training sessions per month, 
   WhatsApp support, and weekly check-ins. Teachers love it because their job gets easier."
```

#### Close (3 min)
```
"So, let me ask you directly: If I could prove these benefits are real—by showing you 
actual data from schools using this—would it make sense to move forward?

[PAUSE for response]

Here's what I suggest: Let's schedule a 30-minute call with your Finance Head + IT person 
for Monday or Tuesday. We'll talk numbers (ROI calculator), timeline, and next steps. 

Do Monday 10 AM or Tuesday 2 PM work better for you?"

[If YES → Calendar invite sent in email]
[If NO → "What's the one concern holding you back?"]
[If UNSURE → "Let me send you the ROI calculator. Review it, and let's chat Friday"]
```

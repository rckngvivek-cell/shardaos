## COST COMPONENTS EXPLAINED

### 1. Admin Time Savings

**Current State (Manual/Excel):**
- Attendance: 6 hrs/week (manually entering from paper registers)
- Grading: 4 hrs/week (consolidating from multiple teachers)
- Reports: 8 hrs/term (= ~2 hours/week) for compliance
- Scheduling: 3 hrs/week (class scheduling, room allocation)
- **Total: 21 hours/week of admin burden**

**With Our Platform:**
- Attendance: 15 min/day teacher entry (auto-consolidated)
- Grading: Real-time entry (no consolidation needed)
- Reports: 30-second auto-generation
- Scheduling: AI-assisted (one click)
- **Total: 8-10 hours/week**

**Saved: 11-13 hours/week × ₹400/hr × 52 weeks = ₹2.3-2.7L/year**

### 2. Parent Call Reduction

**Current State:**
- 40-50 parent calls/day asking about:
  - "What's my child's grade?" (30%)
  - "Why was my child absent?" (40%)
  - "When is the next test?" (20%)
  - "Fee status?" (10%)
- **Average call: 5-8 minutes**
- **Staff time: 40 calls × 6 min ÷ 60 = 4 hours/day = 1,000 hours/year**
- **Cost: 1,000 hrs × ₹300/hr = ₹3L/year**

**With Our Platform:**
- Parent portal self-serves 70% of these (no call needed)
- 15 calls/day instead of 40
- Saved: ₹2.1L/year

### 3. Avoided IT Staff Cost

**Traditional ERP Setup:**
- Requires: 1 dedicated IT person
- **Salary @ ₹5L/year** (minimum qualification for PowerSchool)
- Responsibilities: Server maintenance, backups, security patches, user support
- Plus: Server/infrastructure @ ₹1.5L/year

**Our Platform (Cloud):**
- Zero IT staff needed = **Save ₹5L salary cost**
- Zero infrastructure = **Save ₹1.5L**
- Our support handles all backend

### 4. Avoided Incident Costs

**Data Loss Scenarios (PowerSchool users reported):**
- Server crash during peak season = 2 weeks lost data recovery = ₹5L cost
- Ransomware attack = ₹10-50L ransom + recovery
- Manual backup incomplete = Data lost forever

**Our Platform:**
- Automatic daily backups (never lose data)
- Redundancy across regions
- Ransomware-proof (zero on-premise risk)

**Benefit: Avoid even 1 incident/5 years = ₹2-5L savings**

## KEY MESSAGING PER MODULE

### 1. DASHBOARD
**Pain Addressed:** "I don't know what's happening in my school without 5 emails"
- ✅ Real-time view of all classes
- ✅ Exception alerts (>5 absences today? → Alert!)
- ✅ One-click drill-down to individual students

### 2. REPORTING (The Cost Saver)
**Pain Addressed:** "Generating term reports takes 2 weeks + errors"
- ✅ 20 pre-built reports (Board compliance, Performance, Finance)
- ✅ Generate in seconds (not hours)
- ✅ No Excel formulae mistakes
- ✅ Auto-email to stakeholders (parents, board)

### 3. PARENT PORTAL (The Call Reducer)
**Pain Addressed:** "40 parent calls/day just asking about grades"
**Cost: ₹5L/year in staff time**
- ✅ Parents self-serve (No school involvement)
- ✅ Real-time updates (Grades within seconds of teacher entry)
- ✅ Secure + audit trail
- ✅ Reduces calls by 70-80%

### 4. STUDENT APP (The Engagement Tool)
**Pain Addressed:** "Students don't care about school announcements"
- ✅ Always-on access (students check grades immediately)
- ✅ Notifications (grades, events, homework)
- ✅ Improves attendance (students know status)
- ✅ Reduces "I didn't know" excuses

### 5. SMS ALERTS (The Compliance Tool)
**Pain Addressed:** "Parents miss announcements. No communication records."
- ✅ Automatic SMS (No manual copying)
- ✅ Compliance record (CBSE/ICSE audits require proof of communication)
- ✅ 98% open rate (SMS vs Email 20%)

### 6. ANALYTICS (The Decision Tool)
**Pain Addressed:** "I make decisions on gut feeling, not data"
- ✅ Identify at-risk students (early intervention)
- ✅ Performance by subject (improve weak areas)
- ✅ Teacher effectiveness metrics
- ✅ NPS tracking (satisfaction trends)

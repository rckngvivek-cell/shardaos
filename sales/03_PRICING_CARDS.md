## NEGOTIATION TACTICS

### If budget is tight:
- **Offer 20% discount** for 2-year commitment
- **Offer 15% discount** for 18-month commitment
- **Bundle multiple schools** from same organization (10-15% discount)

### If they want more features:
- **Suggest upgrade path:** Start Tier 1 → upgrade to Tier 3 after 6 months
- **Offer feature add-ons:** ₹50K/year for white-label, ₹30K/year for advanced analytics
- **Seasonal pricing:** Lower rates (₹200K) for summer pilot → full price in Sept

### If they hesitate:
- **Milestone pricing:** Pay ₹100K upfront, ₹50K after 30 days, ₹50K after 60 days
- **Performance guarantee:** Full refund if not 80+ hours/month saved by day 45
- **Expansion clause:** Lock current price for 3 years even if list price increases

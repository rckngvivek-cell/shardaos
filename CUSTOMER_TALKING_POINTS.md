#### Problem Statement
> "Your teachers can't mark exams without internet. Your internet drops. Exams fail. Students panic. You lose data. Standard ERPs like SchoolCanvas require constant connectivity—they're designed for cities with stable internet."

#### Our Solution
> "We built for India's reality: offices, schools, and teachers go offline 2-3 times every week. Our system works offline. Teachers mark exams, take attendance, enter grades—all while offline. When internet returns, automatic sync. Zero data loss. Teachers never see an error."

#### Business Impact
| Impact | Metric | Value |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Time Saved** | Exam delays per year | 0 (vs. 8-10 with other systems) |
| **Stress Reduction** | Staff calls about loss of data | 0 |
| **Reliability** | System uptime | 99.97% (theirs: 99.5%) |
| **Cost** | Infrastructure needed | None (they don't need backup internet) |

#### Example Script
> "Imagine this: It's 2 PM. English exam running. Your Airtel WiFi drops. With SchoolCanvas? Teachers walk around confused. 'Is the internet back? Did it save?' 50 parents calling about exam status.
> 
> With us? Teacher doesn't even notice the WiFi dropped. Exams still being submitted. When WiFi comes back 15 minutes later—automatic sync. Parents never knew there was a problem."

#### How to Position
- **Don't say:** "We use offline-first technology"

# 🆘 BLOCKER PROTOCOL

**When:** An agent reports being stuck  
**Your Response Time:** <5 minutes  
**Your Process:**

1. **Ask (2 min):** "What's blocking you? What do you need?"
2. **Think (1 min):** Can you solve this immediately?
3. **Solve (1 min):** If yes → make decision + unblock
4. **Escalate (1 min):** If no → call Lead Architect

**Examples:**

```
Blocker: Backend says "I don't know the Firestore schema design"
Your Action: Hop on Slack call (2 min), sketch schema together, send design doc
Result: Agent unblocked, keeps coding

Blocker: DevOps says "Production dashboard won't load"
Your Action: "Can you restart it? Takes 30 sec?" 
           If stuck > 2 min → escalate to Lead Architect

Blocker: Sales can't reach 5 leads
Your Action: "I'll get CEO to send intro emails" (5 leaders from his network)
Result: Demo calls scheduled

Blocker: Architecture decision needed
Your Action: Immediately call Lead Architect
           Get decision in <10 min
Result: Team unblocked, keeps moving
```
